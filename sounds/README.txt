Plants vs Zombies - Sound Effects

Required sound files (WAV format):
- plant_place.wav    - Sound when placing a plant
- zombie_groan.wav   - Zombie spawn/groan sound
- chomp.wav          - Attack/bite sound effect
- sun_collect.wav    - Collecting sun points
- victory.wav        - Win game fanfare
- defeat.wav         - Lose game sound
- button_click.wav   - UI button click
- plant_attack.wav   - Plant attack sound

Download free CC0 sound effects from:
- https://opengameart.org/content/512-sound-effects-8-bit-style
- https://opengameart.org/content/cc0-sounds-library
- https://freesound.org (filter by CC0 license)

The game will work without sound files - audio is optional.
